# Tactical Plays & Motifs
- **Fork:** A single piece attacks two or more enemy pieces simultaneously (commonly done by Knights).
- **Pin:** An attacked piece cannot move without exposing a more valuable piece behind it to capture.
- **Skewer:** An attack on a valuable piece that forces it to move, exposing a less valuable piece behind it.
- **Battery:** Lining up two or more pieces (like Rooks or a Queen and Bishop) on the same file or diagonal to multiply attacking power.
- **Discovered Attack:** Moving a piece out of the way to reveal an attack from a piece behind it.
