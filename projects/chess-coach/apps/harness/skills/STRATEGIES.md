# Positional Strategies
- **Center Control:** The most important early goal. Pieces in the center control more squares and can pivot to attack or defend quickly.
- **King Safety:** Castle early. Do not push the pawns in front of your castled King unless absolutely necessary.
- **Piece Development:** Do not move the same piece twice in the opening. Get Knights and Bishops out before the Queen.
- **Pawn Structure:** Avoid doubled pawns (two pawns on the same file) and isolated pawns (pawns with no friendly pawns on adjacent files), as they are weak and hard to defend.
